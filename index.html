How It Works

Farmers request transport

Struggling farmers submit transport requests through Nomastics, detailing their produce and location.

Transport sources offer assistance

Transport providers review requests, offering their help to transport the goods from farm to market

Agreement on terms

Farmers and transport sources agree on fees and schedules, ensuring a fair deal for both parties.

Transport and delivery

Chosen transport sources collect the produce and deliver it safely to the intended market.

After successful delivery, farmers pay for the service. Nomastics handles payment processing and allows both parties to leave valuable reviews.
